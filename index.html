Our Profile

Latar Belakang

RAKYAT University is a higher education institution committed to building the future of the nation through quality, inclusive, and relevant education to the needs of the times.

RAKYAT University opens the widest possible access for the young generation of Indonesia to develop their potential in various fields of science, ranging from technology, business, education, to social and culture.

We believe that education is not just about getting a degree, but about shaping character, honing skills, and creating a real impact on society. Therefore, we prioritize a collaborative, innovative, and real-world practice-oriented learning approach.
